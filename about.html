About Us

We believe managing money should be simple, private, and accessible to everyone.

🌟

Our Mission

Budget Planner was created with one simple goal — to make personal finance easy and stress-free for every Indian family, student, or working professional, regardless of age or tech experience.

We believe you shouldn't need a finance degree to understand your money. Our tool uses plain language, Indian number formats (lakh, crore, thousand), and a clean step-by-step layout so that anyone — from a teenager to a grandparent — can use it confidently.

💡

Why We Built This

Most budgeting apps are complex, require sign-ups, send your data to remote servers, or are built for Western markets. We saw a gap — Indian families needed something different:

  • 🇮🇳 Built specifically for Indian users — Rupees, lakhs, crores
  • 🔒 100% private — data never leaves your browser, no server storage
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Designed for all ages — large text, clear steps, simple language
  • 📵 No ads, no account, no subscription — completely free
  • ⚡ Works instantly in any browser — no app download needed

🛠️

How It Works (Technically)

Budget Planner is a purely browser-based application. Everything — your income, expenses, goals, and chat history — is stored in your browser's Local Storage. This means:

  • 📂 Your data stays on your device at all times
  • 🚫 We have no database — nothing is sent to any server
  • 🔄 Your data persists when you refresh — it's saved locally
  • 🗑️ You can clear all data anytime using browser settings

💡 Tip: Since data is in your browser, switching to a different browser or device will start fresh. Use the same browser to keep your data.
