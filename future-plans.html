Already Live — Version 1.0

Income & Expense Tracking

Add monthly income, categorize expenses into 6 categories, delete entries, view running totals.

✅ Done

50/30/20 Budget Analysis

Visual comparison of your actual spending vs. the recommended Needs/Wants/Savings split.

Financial Health Score

A 0–100 score based on your savings rate, expense pressure, and spending habits.

Savings Forecast

Project your savings 1 month, 1 year, and 5 years into the future.

Goal Planner

Set a savings goal and timeframe — see exactly how much to save per month.

Rule-based chat bot that understands Indian amounts (lakh, crore, thousand), handles typos, stores chat history, and covers 20+ topics.

Local Data Storage

All data — income, expenses, chat — saved to your browser. No account needed.
